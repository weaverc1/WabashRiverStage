Local Information & History

Site Information

This monitoring location is at the former Hutsonville Power Plant (now closed). The site was previously operated by Central Illinois Public Service and continues to provide critical river stage data for flood forecasting and public safety.

Historical Flood Events

April 2013 Flood: During the April 2013 flood event at Hutsonville, significant measurement discrepancies were discovered between readings taken at the State Road 154 bridge and this location. On April 24, the SR 154 readings were nearly 2 feet lower than the Power Plant reading, highlighting the importance of monitoring multiple gauge locations along the river.

Data Availability: Forecasts are issued as needed during times of high water but are not routinely available at this gauge. Detailed stream discharge and gauge height archives from the U.S. Geological Survey are not available for this specific location.
